Who we are我们是谁

This website is operated by the Sustainability Committee of the German Chamber of Commerce, Hong Kong (GCC), 19/F COFCO Tower, 262 Gloucester Road, Causeway Bay, Hong Kong SAR. Contact: gcc-sustainability@hongkong.ahk.de.本网站由香港德国商会（GCC）可持续发展委员会运营，地址：香港铜锣湾告士打道262号中粮大厦19楼。联系方式：gcc-sustainability@hongkong.ahk.de。

What data we collect and why我们收集哪些数据及原因

  • Briefing subscription: your email address, used solely to send you our regulatory briefing. Legal basis: your consent, given via the sign-up checkbox.简报订阅：您的电子邮箱地址，仅用于向您发送法规简报。法律依据：您通过订阅勾选框给予的同意。
  • Account sign-in: your email address, used to operate passwordless sign-in and to determine your access level (registered / member).账户登录：您的电子邮箱地址，用于无密码登录及确定您的访问级别（注册/会员）。
  • Interactive tools (regulation check, CBAM calculator): your inputs are processed in your browser and are not stored by us.互动工具（法规查询、CBAM计算器）：您的输入仅在您的浏览器中处理，我们不会存储。

Who processes the data数据由谁处理

We use the following service providers as processors: Vercel Inc. (website hosting), Sanity AS (content management — no personal data), Resend Inc. (email delivery) and Supabase Inc. (account sign-in). Data may be processed on servers outside Hong Kong and the EU. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes.我们使用以下服务商作为数据处理者：Vercel Inc.（网站托管）、Sanity AS（内容管理——不涉及个人数据）、Resend Inc.（邮件发送）和 Supabase Inc.（账户登录）。数据可能在香港及欧盟以外的服务器上处理。我们不会将您的数据出售或分享给第三方用于其自身目的。

Retention and your rights数据保留与您的权利

We keep your email address until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your account. Under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance and, where applicable, the EU GDPR, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing gcc-sustainability@hongkong.ahk.de. Every briefing email contains an unsubscribe option.我们将保留您的邮箱地址，直至您退订或要求删除账户。根据香港《个人资料（私隐）条例》及（如适用）欧盟GDPR，您可随时发送邮件至 gcc-sustainability@hongkong.ahk.de，要求查阅、更正或删除您的个人资料。每封简报邮件均包含退订选项。

Cookies and trackingCookie与跟踪

This site sets no advertising or tracking cookies. Your language preference and sign-in session are stored locally in your browser. Images and fonts are loaded from our own servers and Google Fonts.本网站不设置任何广告或跟踪Cookie。您的语言偏好和登录会话仅存储在您的浏览器本地。图片和字体从我们自己的服务器及Google Fonts加载。
